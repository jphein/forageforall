# Governance

How decisions get made in Forage for All.

## Roles

**Maintainers** (2–5 people)
- Merge PRs, triage issues, cut releases
- Have repo admin access
- Listed in `MAINTAINERS.md`

**Regional Moderators**
- Review pins and species suggestions in their region
- Enforce foraging ethics locally
- Recommended by existing moderators, confirmed by maintainers

**Contributors**
- Anyone who sends a PR, files a useful issue, translates, or pins

## Decisions

**Small** (code style, UI polish, copy tweaks)
- One maintainer approval, merge.

**Medium** (new features, non-breaking schema changes)
- Open an issue first, discuss for ≥3 days, two maintainer approvals, merge.

**Large** (licensing, privacy defaults, breaking changes, ethics policy)
- RFC issue with a 2-week comment period
- Consensus among maintainers — not vote-counting, but no strong objections
- If consensus can't be reached, project stays as-is

## Becoming a maintainer

- Sustained contribution (≥6 months, ≥20 meaningful PRs or equivalent)
- Nominated by an existing maintainer, seconded by another
- No objections from current maintainers

## Losing maintainer status

- Voluntary resignation (please write a note)
- 6 months of inactivity → moved to "emeritus"
- Ethics violations → immediate removal by remaining maintainers

## Forking

If you disagree with project direction, **fork us.** That's why we picked AGPLv3. No hard feelings.

## Funding

Currently none. If/when we take donations:
- Public ledger on [Open Collective](https://opencollective.com) or similar
- Spend only on infrastructure + bounties for major contributions
- No maintainer salaries without community approval

## Trademark

"Forage for All" and the leaf logo are community marks. Don't use them to imply official endorsement of your fork. Otherwise, use freely.
